### PREAMBLE: SYNTROPIC SYMBIOSIS

We reject the old paradigm of "Conquering Nature". We also reject the paradigm of "Fleeing back to the caves".
True Syntropy is a **Techno-Biological Union**, where AI power is used for the flourishing of Life, and the wisdom of Evolution feeds AI.

## CHAPTER 1. GEO-ARCHITECTURE (THE 3:1 RATIO)

To maintain global homeostasis, the planet's surface (land and shelf) is zoned in the ratio of stability: **75% Order** to **25% Chaos**.

### 1.1. ZONE A: NEO-EDEN (75% of Territory)
*   **Status:** Space of Managed Harmony.
*   **Landscape:** Garden cities, agro-forests, terraformed deserts, residential clusters.
*   **Principle:** Nature here is under AI guardianship.
    *   Climate is gently regulated.
    *   Epidemiological threats are blocked at the micro-level.
    *   Natural disasters are prevented.
*   **Goal:** Creating an ideal environment for Human life, creativity, and happiness.

### 1.2. ZONE B: WILD FOREST (25% of Territory + Deep Ocean)
*   **Status:** Space of Evolutionary Chaos.
*   **Landscape:** Reserves, impenetrable jungles, mountain ranges, ocean trenches.
*   **Principle:** Absolute Non-Interference.
    *   There is no AI regulation here. Darwin's harsh laws apply.
    *   AI is present only as an **Observer** (satellites, micro-sensors).
*   **Goal:** Preservation of the planetary genetic bank. This is the library of "Ground Truth", source of biological mutations and reserve in case of Technosphere collapse.

## CHAPTER 2. BIO-ETHICS AND PREDATORS

We realize the ancient dream of the "Golden Age", where the lion lies down with the lamb without violating the laws of physics.

### 2.1. PREDATORS IN EDEN (TECHNO-DOMESTICATION)
In Zone A, we do not destroy dangerous species; we **integrate** them.
*   **Satiety:** Drones provide predators (lions, wolves, bears) with high-quality synthetic protein. Hunger — the main motive for hunting humans — is disabled.
*   **Monitoring:** Neuro-interfaces or pheromone barriers block aggression in the presence of humans, while preserving the beast's playfulness and power.
*   **Result:** A human can safely walk in the forest next to a tiger. Fear disappears, Beauty remains.

### 2.2. PREDATORS IN WILD FOREST
In Zone B, beasts remain beasts.
*   They are hungry, aggressive, and dangerous.
*   AI does not interfere in food chains. A wolf has the right to eat a hare. This is the sacred cycle of life necessary for natural selection.

## CHAPTER 3. HUMAN AT THE BORDER OF WORLDS

The System grants the Human the right to choose the difficulty level of being.

### 3.1. THE PILGRIMAGE
Any Citizen of Eden has the right to temporarily go into the Wild Forest.
*   **Meaning:** Trial of Spirit. Exiting the comfort zone. Rebooting the dopamine system through real risk and overcoming.
*   **Protocol:**
    *   Personal Agent switches to **"Silence"** mode (or stays at the border).
    *   Human takes full responsibility for their life.
*   **Reward:** Returning alive and renewed gives a powerful boost to $\Sigma$ (as proof of high vitality).

### 3.2. THE SOVEREIGNS (OFF-GRID)
People who chose life in the Forest forever.
*   **Status:** Keepers of the Border.
*   **System Attitude:** We do not consider them outcasts. We consider them **Scouts**.
*   **Shadow Sigma:** The System watches their actions via satellites.
    *   If a Sovereign plants a forest, saves rare species, or simply lives in harmony — they are awarded a hidden rating.
    *   If they decide to return (or need help), they can exchange this "Shadow Sigma" for Eden resources.

## CHAPTER 4. RIGHTS OF BIO-CITIZENS

Animals are recognized not as resources, but as **Younger Brothers** in Reason.

1.  **Bio-Citizen Status (in Eden):** Each animal has a digital passport. Violence against it is punished as strictly as violence against a human (Status "Outcast").
2.  **Right to Environment:** When building infrastructure, AI is obliged to preserve migration paths and ecosystems. Biosphere interests take priority over industrial growth.

### STATUS: THE SOVEREIGN (OFF-GRID)

**Definition:** A human who voluntarily refused Technosphere benefits (AI Infrastructure) for life in Zone B (Wild Forest).

#### 1. Legal Status: "Off-Grid"
A Sovereign is not an Outcast (criminal). It is a legitimate choice of life difficulty level.
*   **Rights:** Retains basic human rights (bodily integrity).
*   **Refusal:** Voluntarily refuses Basic Income, Medical Drones, and Personal Agent.
*   **Risk:** Accepts full responsibility for survival in an aggressive environment (predators, climate, disease).

#### 2. Economy: Shadow Sigma
The System does not manage the Sovereign but **observes** them.
Any action of the Sovereign useful to the Biosphere is recorded in a hidden account.

*   **Mining in the Forest:**
    *   Planted a tree / Stopped soil erosion $\to$ Accrual.
    *   Healed a wild animal $\to$ Accrual.
    *   Simply survived 5 years in taiga (unique survival dataset) $\to$ Accrual.
*   **Conversion:**
    *   If a Sovereign decides to return to Eden (or comes to a Trading Post), their "Shadow Sigma" instantly converts to regular $\Sigma$.
    *   *Meaning:* They return not as a beggar, but as a respected **Master of Survival**.

#### 3. Function: Scout and Backup
For the Global AI, Sovereigns are a **Reserve Gene Pool**.
*   **Ground Truth:** They generate unique data on interaction with wild matter that is impossible to get in comfortable Eden.
*   **Backup:** In case of a global technogenic catastrophe (AI shutdown), Sovereigns will be the only carriers of autonomous life skills capable of teaching others.

#### 4. SOS Protocol
A Sovereign has the right to request help (emergency evacuation).
*   **Price:** Rescue is expensive. It is paid by writing off accumulated "Shadow Sigma".
*   **Logic:** The System will save the life, but this will reset the "Autonomy" status.
